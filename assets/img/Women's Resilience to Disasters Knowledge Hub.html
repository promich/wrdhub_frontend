The WRD Framework

The Women's Resilience to Disasters Framework provides the architecture for building women's disaster and climate resilience and for organising the WRD Knowledge Hub resources into topics.

Three Pillars

Pillar A

Risk

 1. Gender dimensions of disasters 2. Intersectionality 3.Gender data 4.Gender-responsive risk assessment 5. Gender-responsive needs assessment
Pillar B

Systems

 6. Legislation, policies, strategies, plans & projects 7. Budgets, expenditure & funding 8. Information management systems 9. Early warning, preparedness & risk communication systems 10. Coordination mechanisms, networks, & partnerships
Pillar C

Action

 11. Access to finance 12. Access to services 13. Access to resilient infrastructure 14. Women’s resilient livelihoods & businesses 15. Women’s freedom from violence

Women's leadership, voice, agency, participation & advocacy Global frameworks Capabilities, awareness, knowledge & skills
